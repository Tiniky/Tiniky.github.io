RIOT GAMES

About the company

Let's jump back in time to the early 2000s. One day in a USC dorm, two business students started talking about the video game industry. That unintantional talk led to the nowadays well known Riot Games.

In 2006 Brandon Beck and Marc Merill made the first step, they've officially created the Riot Games company. They agreed on making free games while creating ingame contents which can be obtained by spending some money but the main game is enjoyable without them.

They were looking for people who share their passions and video game affection. Only a little group was working for 4 months on the first game's prototype. This game came out in April of 2009, it's called League of Legends. It only had 17 choosable champ. Today it has over 180 million active monthly players and 159 choosable champ.

In 2019 they started working on more projects. First we heard about the Project A which later became Valorant. That year was created the Riot Forge, which is a part of Riot Games, that is focused on the company's other game ideas. Their goal is to release new games each year and to let the players have a deeper look in the magical world of Runeterra.
